Major Milestones in Space Exploration

  1. The Space Race

    The 20th century witnessed an intense rivalry between the United States and the Soviet Union in their pursuit of space exploration supremacy.

    • 1957: The Soviet Union launches Sputnik 1, the first artificial satellite
    • 1961: Yuri Gagarin becomes the first human in space
    • 1969: NASA's Apollo 11 mission lands the first humans on the Moon

  2. Commercial Space industry

    After nearly 40 years the space industry has been revitalized by private companies developing technology to open again the path to the cosmos.

    • 2000-2002: Creation of private space companies such as SpaceX and Blue Orgin.
    • 2010: First Falcon 9 succesful flight.
    • 2020: SpaceX is the first commerical space company to send humans to space.

Space Exploration

Ever since humanity's first venture into space, we have been driven by an insatiable curiosity to explore the vast unknown. From the initial steps on the Moon to the ongoing missions to Mars and beyond, space exploration has captured our collective imagination and pushed the boundaries of scientific knowledge.
